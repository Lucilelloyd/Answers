Webroot SecureAnywhere Download
===============================

Learn how to download Webroot SecureAnywhere on your device.

1. Open your web browser  
2. Go to the official Webroot or Geek Squad download page  
3. Locate the Webroot SecureAnywhere download option  
4. Click on the download button  
5. Enter your Geek Squad keycode if prompted  
6. Save the installer file to your device  
7. Wait for the download to complete  
8. Navigate to your Downloads folder  
9. Verify the setup file is successfully downloaded
